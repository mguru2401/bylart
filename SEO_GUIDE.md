## 🚀 Post-Deployment Steps (IMPORTANT!)

### Step 1: Google Search Console
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Add your property: `https://bylart.com`
3. Verify ownership (use HTML tag method or DNS)
4. Submit your sitemap: `https://bylart.com/sitemap.xml`
5. Request indexing for main pages

### Step 2: Google Business Profile
1. Create a [Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/business/)
2. Add your business details
3. Verify your business
4. Add photos, services, and contact information

### Step 3: Google Analytics
1. Set up [Google Analytics 4](https://analytics.google.com/)
2. Add tracking code to your website
3. Monitor traffic and user behavior

### Step 4: Schema Markup (Structured Data)
Add JSON-LD structured data to your pages:
```html
<script type="application/ld+json">
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "HealthAndBeautyBusiness",
  "name": "BYLART",
  "description": "Bach Flower Therapy platform for emotional wellness",
  "url": "https://bylart.com",
  "logo": "https://bylart.com/34.png",
  "sameAs": [
    "https://www.facebook.com/bylart",
    "https://www.instagram.com/bylart",
    "https://twitter.com/bylart"
  ]
}
</script>
```
